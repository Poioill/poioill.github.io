Snake

The purpose of the Snake game is to control a snake on a grid, guiding it to eat food while avoiding collisions with the snake's own body and the game boundaries. The objective is to grow the snake by eating food, earning points, and challenging oneself to achieve a higher score without colliding with obstacles.
